# Библиотека сцен — Мия

20 типовых сцен для обложек, соцсетей, клипов. Использовать с reference image эталона (см. [05-nano-banana-workflow.md](05-nano-banana-workflow.md)).

## Шаблон базового промпта

```
This is Miya. Same exact character — same face, same eyes, same hair, same
vertical seam on her left cheek, same thin black cable behind her right ear,
same cyan glowing veins. Keep her exact identity.

Scene: [сцена ниже]

Style: hyper-realistic cinematic photography, dark synthwave atmosphere,
deep navy blue + cyan + magenta color palette, moody lighting, shallow depth
of field, film grain, 8K detail.
```

## Портретные (обложки синглов, аватары)

### 1. Прямой портрет, неоновый бэклайт
> Extreme close-up portrait, shoulders up. Looking directly at camera with a calm, melancholic expression. Cold cyan rim light from behind creating a halo around her hair. Subtle magenta accent on her left cheek. Dark gradient background with atmospheric haze.

### 2. Профиль, дым и циан
> Side profile portrait, head turned to the right. Soft volumetric fog around her. Strong cyan rim light from the left, magenta backlight. Her hair falls naturally over her shoulder. Distant.

### 3. Половина лица в тени
> Close-up portrait, face half-illuminated. One side bathed in deep cyan light, the other side completely dark with only a magenta accent on the cheekbone. Direct eye contact. Intense, unreadable expression.

### 4. Глаза крупным планом, активен глитч
> Extreme macro close-up of her eyes. Her left eye shows subtle digital glitch artifacts, chromatic aberration around the iris, faint pixel fragmentation near the eyelashes. Dark gray-blue iris with intricate detail. Cinematic lighting.

### 5. Сквозь стекло с дождём
> Close-up portrait shot through a wet glass window with rain droplets and water trails. She is on the other side of the glass, slightly blurred, illuminated by neon city lights behind her — cyan and magenta reflections. Melancholic expression.

## Перформанс (для клипов и постов)

### 6. На сцене с микрофоном
> Full body shot, performing on a dark industrial stage. She holds a vintage condenser microphone. Smoke fills the stage. Strong cyan spotlight from above, magenta wash from the sides. Crowd is invisible in the darkness below. Cinematic concert photography.

### 7. В студии у пульта
> Medium shot, sitting at a futuristic mixing console with multiple glowing screens. Wearing oversized dark techwear hoodie. Headphones around her neck. Cyan light from the monitors illuminates her face. Magenta accent light from the side.

### 8. Запись вокала в кабинке
> Medium close-up, standing in a soundproof vocal booth. A studio condenser microphone in front of her. She wears headphones. Hand slightly raised. Pop filter visible. Warm low light mixed with cool cyan from a monitor outside the booth.

## Атмосферные (контент, истории, тизеры)

### 9. Крыша ночью, дождь
> Wide shot, standing alone on the edge of a rooftop at night. Heavy rain. Neon city skyline behind her — towering signs in cyan and magenta. Her silhouette is illuminated from behind. Wearing an oversized dark techwear jacket, hood down.

### 10. Заброшенное метро
> Wide shot, walking down an abandoned subway tunnel. Flickering fluorescent lights. Wet concrete floor reflecting cyan tube lights. She is small in the frame, walking away from the camera into the darkness.

### 11. Пустой ночной город под фонарём
> Medium-wide shot, standing under a single streetlight on an empty wet street at 3 AM. Fog drifts low. Distant neon signs out of focus in the background. Her hands in her pockets. Looking down.

### 12. Криокапсула
> Medium shot, lying inside a futuristic cryo-capsule with transparent curved glass. Soft cyan light glows from inside. Magenta status indicators around the capsule. Her eyes closed. Cables connected to her temples and arms.

### 13. Орбитальная станция, окно с видом на Землю
> Medium shot, standing in front of a large circular window of a space station. Earth visible below, partially in shadow. Cool blue planetary light illuminates her face. Magenta indicator lights from instruments behind her. She presses her hand against the glass.

### 14. Серверная комната
> Wide shot, standing in a long corridor of server racks. Rows of blinking cyan and magenta LEDs stretch into the distance on both sides. She is small in the frame. Soft volumetric haze in the air.

### 15. Уход в неоновый коридор
> Wide shot from behind. She walks away from the camera down a long narrow corridor lit entirely by alternating cyan and magenta neon strips on the walls. Wet floor reflecting the lights.

## Эмоциональные (драматические моменты)

### 16. Слёзы и глитч
> Extreme close-up portrait. A single tear runs down her cheek, catching the cyan light. Her left eye shows active digital glitch — chromatic aberration, slight pixel distortion. Expression of quiet devastation. Magenta backlight.

### 17. Гнев, искры от вен
> Medium close-up. She clenches her jaw, looking off-camera with intensity. Her cyan veins glow brighter than usual under the skin of her neck and collarbone. Small magenta signal sparks float near her shoulders. Cold harsh lighting.

### 18. Лежит, провод выдернут
> Medium shot from above. She lies on a cold metal floor on her side. The thin black cable that normally connects behind her ear is disconnected, dangling. Faint cyan light from her veins is dimmer than usual. Magenta puddle of light around her. Expression of exhaustion or shutdown.

### 19. Касается своего отражения
> Medium shot, standing in front of a tall mirror. She presses her hand against the glass. Her reflection presses back. Reflection is subtly different — slightly more glitched, slightly more haunted. Cyan light from above, magenta from behind.

### 20. Поёт, магента-артефакты в воздухе
> Medium close-up, eyes closed, singing into a microphone. Magenta signal artifacts and small floating particles drift in the air around her mouth and silhouette. Cyan rim light. Atmospheric haze. The most "alive" moment of the character.

## Чек-лист для каждой сцены

- [ ] Якоря идентичности видны (шов, глаз, кабель, вены)
- [ ] Палитра выдержана (navy / cyan / magenta)
- [ ] Не активировано больше 2 эмоциональных эффектов одновременно
- [ ] Соответствие настроению трека / поста
- [ ] Финальный формат (квадрат для обложки, 9:16 для сторис, 16:9 для YouTube)
